Frequently Asked Questions

How do you handle rentals during the winter months?

We use a specialized brine solution in the waste tanks to prevent freezing during the winter. For events, we strongly recommend our heated restroom trailers to ensure the comfort of your guests in freezing temperatures.

What happens if the unit gets full before the scheduled service?

If usage exceeds expectations, simply call our office. We can schedule an extra pump-out service or deliver additional units to your site to handle the increased capacity.

Are your units compliant with health standards?

Absolutely. All our portable toilets and trailers meet or exceed USA's state health and safety codes. We regularly update our inventory and training to stay in full compliance with all local regulations.

Do I need a permit to place a porta potty?

Permit requirements vary by town and specific location. If you plan to place the unit on private property, a permit is usually not required. However, placement on a public street or sidewalk often requires municipal approval. We can help guide you through this process.

Are your units suitable for children?

Yes, our units are user-friendly for all ages. For events with many children, we recommend adding extra handwashing stations and ensuring units are placed on flat, stable ground for easy access.
